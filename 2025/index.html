4th International Conference "Challenges and Reality of the IT-space: Software Engineering and Cybersecurity"

(SECS-2025) Thursday, 23 October 2025 – Friday, 24 October 2025
Kyiv, Ukraine (online)

Call for papers(current) Committee Registration and Submission Important dates Proceedings Archives Contact Information

The Center for Cyber Intelligence and Security Innovations (Ukraine), University of Custom and Finance (Ukraine), State University of Trade and Economics (Ukraine), ITBIZ LLC (Ukraine), Poznań University of Economics and Business (Poland), University of Salford (UK), La Salle University (U.S.), National University of Life and Environmental Sciences of Ukraine (Ukraine), University of Economics and Law “KROK” (Ukraine), Institute of Social and Economic Development (Ukraine), and OÜ “Scientific Center of Innovative Research” (Estonia) invite researchers and industry professionals to join our discussion platform. This initiative aims to foster international collaboration and address contemporary and future challenges in software engineering and cybersecurity.

Conference topics:

  1. Innovations in Cybersecurity Systems
    • Cyber Resilience Strategies for National Infrastructure Protection
    • Advancements in Cybersecurity Education and Workforce Development
    • AI and Machine Learning Applications in Cyber Defense
    • Zero Trust Architecture for Network Security
    • Social Engineering Threats and the Human Factor in Cybersecurity
    • Securing Internet of Things (IoT) and Ubiquitous Systems
    • Ransomware and Denial of Service Attacks: Prevention and Response
  2. Cyberdiplomacy in Navigating Security and Sovereignty Challenges
    • The Role of Cyberdiplomacy in Economic Stability and Security
    • Ethical Considerations in National and International Cybersecurity Operations
    • Global Collaboration and Challenges in Cybersecurity Efforts
    • Tackling Fake News and Disinformation on a Global Scale
    • Election Security and Integrity in Cross-Border Context
    • Data Sovereignty and Cybersecurity in International Relations
    • Cybersecurity’s Impact on Geopolitical Alliances and Policy
  3. Software Engineering for Enhanced Security and Privacy
    • Best Practices in Secure Software Development
    • Privacy by Design: Integrating Privacy into Software Systems
    • Threat Modeling for Security-Driven Software Development
    • Secure Web Design and Programming Techniques
    • Database Security and Management for Sensitive Information
    • IoT Integration in Secure Software Development
    • AI’s Role in Secure and Private Software Engineerin
  4. Leveraging AI for Sustainable Digital Transformation
    • Ethical AI Implementation in Digital Transformation Initiatives
    • AI Governance: Policies for Responsible Digital Evolution
    • Bias Mitigation in AI Model, Algorithms and Applications
    • AI-Driven Decision Making: Ensuring Accountability
    • AI and Human Collaboration in Digital Workplace
    • AI in education
